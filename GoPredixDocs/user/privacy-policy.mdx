PrediX ("PrediX", "we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. ## 2. Scope and Applicability

This Policy applies to information we collect through the Services and other online properties that link to this Policy. It does not apply to information collected offline or by third parties not described here. Terms that are not defined in this Policy have the meaning given in our Terms of Use.

## 3. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, information collected automatically, and information from third parties as described below.

### 3.1 Information You Provide Directly

- **Wallet address:** Public blockchain wallet addresses you connect to PrediX.
- **Email address:** Provided when you register, subscribe, or otherwise communicate with us.
- **Support and correspondence:** Messages you send to support (support@gopredix.xyz) and any information contained in such messages.
- **Optional profile fields:** Any profile information you choose to add (e.g., username, bio, profile picture).

**Important:** We do not request or collect private keys, seed phrases, or other secrets that would allow us to access your wallets.

### 3.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Cookies and similar technologies:** Information collected by cookies, local storage, web beacons, and similar tools (see Section 7).
- **Usage and device data:** Pages visited, session duration, browser and device type, operating system, IP address, referral source, and other diagnostic or performance data.
- **Aggregated/anonymous data:** We may create aggregated or de-identified data from the information we collect; such data is not personal data.

### 3.3 Information from Third Parties

- **Integration providers:** If you sign in or connect through third-party services or connect to third-party platforms, we may receive information those services provide (subject to their terms).
- **Public blockchain data:** Transactions and on-chain public data associated with your connected wallet address.

## 4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for purposes necessary to operate, maintain, secure, and improve the Services, and as otherwise permitted by law. Typical uses include:

- Enabling wallet-based authentication and participation in prediction markets.
- Maintaining, operating and improving the Services, including analytics, troubleshooting and product development.
- Preventing fraud, abuse, and security incidents and enforcing our Terms of Use.
- Communicating service notices, security alerts, and support responses.
- Providing promotional notices, surveys, and updates where you have consented to receive marketing communications.
- Complying with legal obligations.

We will only process personal information for purposes compatible with the purposes at or before the time of collection or as permitted by applicable law.

While PrediX does not sell or trade personal information, we may disclose personal information in the following limited circumstances:

- **Service providers and vendors:** Third-party providers that perform services on our behalf (e.g., email delivery, analytics, customer support, verification, and technical infrastructure).

**Note:** Blockchain transaction data associated with your wallet address is publicly visible on the relevant blockchain and is beyond our control.
