# Place Predictions

Learn how to trade predictions by buying and selling shares.

## Finding Markets

### Browse Markets

1. Click "Markets" in navigation
2. See all active markets
3. Use filters:
   - **Category** - Finance, Sports, Politics, Tech
   - **Status** - Active, Locked, Resolved
   - **Deadline** - Soon, This Month, Later
   - **Sort** - Volume, Deadline, New

### Search Markets

1. Click "Search"
2. Enter keywords
3. Results show matching markets
4. Click market to view

### Market Details

Before trading, review:
- Question clarity
- All outcomes listed
- Deadline date and time
- Current volume
- Current prices

## Buying Shares

### How It Works

When you buy shares:
- You pay the current price
- You own shares of that outcome
- If outcome wins, you profit
- If outcome loses, you lose your investment

### Step-by-Step

**Step 1: Select Market**
1. Go to Markets
2. Click market you are interested in
3. Review details

**Step 2: Choose Outcome**
1. See all outcomes listed
2. Pick one you believe in
3. Click to expand

**Step 3: Set Amount**
1. Enter number of shares
2. See price per share
3. Total cost calculated
4. See slippage estimate

**Step 4: Review**
1. Check total cost
2. Verify you have enough BNB (gas)
3. Review slippage tolerance
4. Confirm details

**Step 5: Confirm**
1. Click "Buy Shares"
2. MetaMask popup appears
3. Review transaction
4. Click "Confirm"
5. Wait 5-30 seconds

**Step 6: Verify**
1. See confirmation message
2. Transaction hash provided
3. Shares appear in Portfolio
4. You now own the position

## Understanding Prices

### Price Mechanics

**Price equals Market Probability**
- Price 0.70 equals 70 percent chance of winning
- Price 0.30 equals 30 percent chance of winning

**Why prices matter:**
- Lower price equals better value
- Higher price equals crowd agrees
- You profit if outcome exceeds entry price

### Slippage

Price impact of your trade:

```
Small buy (1 to 100 shares): less than 0.5 percent slippage
Large buy (1000 plus shares): 1 to 5 percent slippage
Huge buy (10000 plus shares): 5 percent plus slippage
```

**Manage slippage:**
1. Set max slippage tolerance (default 1 percent)
2. Break large trades into smaller ones
3. Buy at off peak hours
4. Check market liquidity first

## Selling Shares

### When to Sell

Reasons to sell:

1. **Take Profit** - Price went up, lock in gains
2. **Cut Loss** - Price went down, limit damage
3. **Rebalance** - Adjust portfolio
4. **Raise Cash** - Need liquidity
5. **Exit Early** - Before market resolves

### How to Sell

**Step 1: Go to Portfolio**
1. Click "Portfolio"
2. See all your positions
3. Find position to sell

**Step 2: Select Position**
1. Click the position
2. See details:
   - Shares owned
   - Entry price
   - Current price
   - Current P and L

**Step 3: Sell**
1. Click "Sell"
2. Enter shares to sell (or "Max")
3. See revenue you will get
4. See gas cost

**Step 4: Confirm**
1. Review total
2. Click "Confirm Sell"
3. Approve in MetaMask
4. Wait for confirmation

**Step 5: Done**
1. Shares sold
2. Cash received in wallet
3. Position updated

### Sell Example

```
Your Position:
- Entry: 100 shares at 0.50 equals 50 BNB
- Current: Same 100 shares at 0.65 equals 65 BNB
- Unrealized Profit: 15 BNB

Sell 100 shares at 0.65:
- Revenue: 65 BNB
- Profit: 15 BNB (30 percent gain)
- Gas Cost: approximately 0.1 BNB
- Net: approximately 14.9 BNB profit
```

## Risk Management

### Position Sizing

- Start small (10 to 50 shares)
- Do not use all capital
- Diversify across markets
- Keep reserves for gas and opportunities

### Dollar Cost Averaging

Buy over time instead of all at once:

```
Instead of: 1000 shares at once
Try: 100 shares and day for 10 days

Benefits:
- Lower average cost
- Reduced slippage
- Better risk management
- Time to evaluate
```

### Stop Loss

Set a price to exit if wrong:

```
Bought at: 0.50
Stop loss: 0.40 (20 percent loss)

If price drops to 0.40:
- Sell automatically
- Limit losses
- Preserve capital
```

## Troubleshooting

### Insufficient Balance

- Not enough BNB for transaction and gas
- Solution: Get more testnet BNB

### Slippage Too High

- Large trade impact
- Solution: Split into smaller trades

### Transaction Failed

- Network congestion
- Wrong gas price
- Solution: Try again in a few minutes

### Wrong Price

- Prices move constantly
- Solution: Set max price tolerance

## Advanced Strategies

### Arbitrage

Buy low in one market, sell high in another

### Hedging

Take opposite positions to reduce risk

### Scaling In and Out

Gradually add and remove positions
