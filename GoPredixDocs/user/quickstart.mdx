# Quick Start Guide

Get up and running with GoPreDix in just 5 minutes!

## Step 1: Connect Your Wallet (1 min)

1. Visit [app.gopredix.com](https://www.gopredix.xyz)
2. Click **"Connect Wallet"**
3. Select **MetaMask**
4. Approve connection request

## Step 2: Set Up BSC Testnet (1 min)

Your MetaMask should automatically add BSC Testnet. If not:

1. Open MetaMask
2. Click network selector (top)
3. Click **"Add Network"**
4. Enter:
   - **Name:** BSC Testnet
   - **RPC:** https://data-seed-prebsc-1-b.binance.org:8545
   - **Chain ID:** 97
   - **Currency:** BNB

## Step 3: Get Testnet Tokens (2 min)

1. Go to [BSC Testnet Faucet](https://testfaucet.binance.org/)
2. Enter your wallet address
3. Request 0.5 testnet BNB
4. Wait ~1 minute for confirmation

## Step 4: Create Your Profile (1 min)

1. Back on GoPreDix, click **"Profile"**
2. Enter:
   - Username
   - Email (optional)
   - Avatar (optional)
3. Click **"Save"**

## Step 5: Place Your First Prediction

### Browse Markets

1. Go to **"Markets"**
2. Find a market you believe in
3. Check the current prices

### Buy Shares

1. Click on a market
2. Select an outcome (e.g., "Yes")
3. Enter amount (try 10 shares)
4. Review price and slippage
5. Click **"Buy"**
6. Approve transaction in MetaMask
7. Wait 5-10 seconds for confirmation

### Monitor Your Position

1. Go to **"Portfolio"**
2. See your position details:
   - Shares owned
   - Entry price
   - Current value
   - Unrealized P&L
