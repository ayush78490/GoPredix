# Manage Your Profile

Set up and customize your GoPreDix profile.

## View Profile

1. Click **Profile** in navigation
2. See your current information
3. View your statistics

## Edit Profile

### Username

Your unique identifier on GoPreDix.

- **Requirements:** 3-20 characters, alphanumeric + underscore
- **Change anytime:** Yes
- **Public:** Yes (visible to other traders)

To update:
1. Click **"Edit"**
2. Enter new username
3. Click **"Save"**

### Email

For notifications and account recovery.

- **Public:** No (private)
- **Required:** No (optional)
- **Verified:** Recommend verifying

To update:
1. Click **"Edit"**
2. Enter new email
3. Click verification link in email
4. Done!

### Profile Picture

Upload a custom avatar (optional).

- **Formats:** JPG, PNG, GIF
- **Size:** Max 5MB
- **Display:** On your profile and trades

To upload:
1. Click **"Edit"**
2. Click **"Upload Image"**
3. Select file
4. Click **"Save"**

### Bio

Short description about you (optional).

- **Character limit:** 160
- **Visible:** On your public profile
- **Example:** "Crypto trader | DeFi enthusiast"

### Display Preferences

#### Theme

- **Light Mode:** Default light background
- **Dark Mode:** Better for night
- **System:** Follow device settings

#### Language

- **English:** Default
- **More languages:** Coming soon

#### Notifications

- **Email Notifications:** ON/OFF
- **Discord Notifications:** Coming soon
- **Market alerts:** ON/OFF

## Account Statistics

Your profile displays:

### Trading Stats

- **Total Predictions:** Number of trades
- **Win Rate:** % of winning trades
- **Total Volume:** Sum of all trades
- **Realized P&L:** Profit/loss from settled trades

### Recent Activity

- Last trades
- Recent markets viewed
- Portfolio changes
