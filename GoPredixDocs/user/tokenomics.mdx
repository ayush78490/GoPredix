### How Staking Works

**Lock your GPX to earn (coming soon):**

```
Stake: 1,000 GPX

Annual Yield: 15 percent
```

### Staking Tiers

| Amount | Reward | Duration |
|--------|--------|----------|

### Staking Process

Coming soon (Q1 2026):

1. Go to **Tokenomics to Staking**
2. Enter amount to stake
3. Select lock period
4. Click **"Stake"**
5. Confirm in wallet
6. Earn rewards!

### Unstaking

1. Click **"Unstake"**
2. Get GPX plus rewards
