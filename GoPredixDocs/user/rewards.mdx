## PrediX Points System

### What are PrediX Points?

**PrediX Points** are monthly rewards earned through activity on GoPreDix.

- Earned every month based on your activity
- Can be accumulated and redeemed
- Non transferable (personal to your account)
- Based on both on chain and off chain activities
- Reset monthly (fresh start each month)

### How You Earn Points

**On Chain Activities:**

- Create markets on blockchain
- Trade shares (buy and sell)
- Settle market outcomes
- Provide liquidity to markets
- Participate in active markets

**Off Chain Activities:**

- Complete tasks on Zealy
- Complete campaigns on Galxe
- Community participation
- Social media engagement
- Educational activities

**Your Monthly Points:**

- Tracked automatically by our system
- Based on your all activities (on chain and off chain)
- Calculation method: Private (backend)
- Visible in your rewards dashboard
- Updated in real time

### Point Accumulation

Your PrediX Points accumulate monthly:

- Every transaction and task generates points
- Points tracked automatically
- See your total in dashboard
- Cannot lose or decrease points
- Carry forward to next month

### Monthly Point Tiers

| Activity Level | Reward Level |
|---|---|
| Casual participant | Bronze |
| Active participant | Silver |
| Very active participant | Gold |
| Power user | Platinum |
| Elite contributor | Diamond |

### Point Distribution Timeline

- **Week 1 to 3:** Points earned in real time
- **Week 4:** Month end calculation
- **Month end:** Points finalized
- **Next month:** New month begins
- **Anytime:** Check your dashboard for total

## FAQ

**Q: How do I start earning points?**
A: Start with on chain trading or complete off chain tasks on Zealy and Galxe.

**Q: What activities earn the most points?**
A: Depends on backend algorithm. Combination of on chain and off chain maximizes rewards.

**Q: Can I do only off chain tasks?**
A: Yes, but combination of both typically accumulates faster.

**Q: How do I join Zealy?**
A: Visit Zealy website, search for GoPreDix campaigns, connect wallet and complete tasks.

**Q: How do I join Galxe?**
A: Visit Galxe website, find GoPreDix campaigns, connect wallet and participate.

**Q: Do off chain tasks require KYC?**
A: Some Galxe campaigns may require identity verification. Check campaign details.

**Q: Can I lose points from off chain tasks?**
A: No, points never decrease. Only increase from new activities.

**Q: How long to get an NFT?**
A: Depends on your activity level (on chain and off chain combined).

**Q: Are Zealy and Galxe rewards separate from PrediX?**
A: You earn on their platforms separately and also earn PrediX Points simultaneously.

**Q: When are off chain points credited?**
A: Points credited monthly at end of calculation period.
